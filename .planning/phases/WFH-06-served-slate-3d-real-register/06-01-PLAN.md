<objective>
Port REAL-01 — the UMAP-linear-radial force-directed layout — from the legacy
`cp/force_layout.js` into the served `fe/projector.mjs` closure factory. Each chunk
fixes a ray from its URL root through its initial UMAP position and may move ONLY
along that ray; pairwise collider repulsion is projected onto each node's own ray
direction (hard floor, zero force above `MIN_SEPARATION`, exact correction below).
This plan also creates the Wave-0 test scaffold the whole phase verifies against:
the new pure-math unit tests in `projector.test.mjs` and the new `force-directed`
e2e block + test hooks in `projector.spec.js` / `editor.html`.

Purpose: REAL-01 is the foundation every later REAL plan animates on top of — the
per-node ray data + force step are consumed by REAL-02 (camera framing reads node
world positions) and REAL-04 (the arrow projects the post-force position). It must
land first and be unit-testable.
Output: `projector.mjs` gains `computeRayDir` (pure export), `_initForceLayout`,
`_computeRayData`, `_stepForceDirected` (closure-internal), and `setNodes(coords,
urlRoots)` signature extension; `projector.test.mjs` gains ray/collider unit tests;
`projector.spec.js` gains the `force-directed` e2e block; `editor.html` gains the
`__mm_proj_set_with_roots` + `__mm_proj_node_positions` test hooks.
</objective>
